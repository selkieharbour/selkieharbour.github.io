Many Nights a Whisper (2025, PC)

Many Nights a Whisper is a meditative third-person archery game we developed alongside Deconstructeam. Train to land an impossibly distant shot to hone your skill for the sacred ceremony that will bring a decade of prosperity, given you succeed.
