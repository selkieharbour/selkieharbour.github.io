Description

Many Nights a Whisper is a short and meditative interactive tale about dreams, pressure, and expectations. This bite-sized experimental piece is around 60 minutes of gameplay time and is designed to be experienced in a single sitting.

Delve into the routine of the Dreamer, the person chosen to perform this demanding ceremony. During the day, train with your slingshot and engage with your mentor in warm and intimate discussions. At night, listen to the townsfolk at the Confession Wall, weigh their whispers, and decide whether to grant their wishes.

History

Together, Deconstructeam and Selkie Harbour have been collaborating since 2022, exploring new storytelling possibilities in 3D through a series of experimental short games. This game marks an exciting step toward developing even more immersive and memorable narrative experiences.

Features

  • Deconstructeam's signature narrative design features ethical conundrums and heartfelt conversations.
  • Selkie Harbour's technical expertise brings to life a colorful low-poly 3D ruin-strewn archipelago inspired by Iberian culture.
  • Through fingerspit's on-brand style, savor a contemplative, piano-driven soundtrack that weaves a dreamy and immersive ambient soundscape.
